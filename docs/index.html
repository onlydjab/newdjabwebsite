About DJ AB — Auburn, AL

Professional DJ in Auburn, AL

DJ AB is a professional DJ and music curator based in Auburn, Alabama, known for commanding every room with an electrifying mix of House, EDM, Hip-Hop, R&B, and Top 40. With a read-the-crowd approach and seamless transitions, every set is a unique experience tailored to the vibe of the moment.

FAQ — Booking DJ AB

What areas does DJ AB serve?

DJ AB is based in Auburn, Alabama and regularly performs across Tuscaloosa, Birmingham, and Auburn. Available for events throughout Alabama, Georgia, Florida, Tennessee, Mississippi, and nationwide with advance notice.

What types of events does DJ AB perform at?

Weddings, private parties, club & bar nights, corporate events, birthdays, graduations, and more. DJ AB adapts to any atmosphere and guest demographic.

How far in advance should I book?

Dates book quickly — especially Fridays and Saturdays during peak seasons. Booking 4–8 weeks in advance is recommended, though last-minute availability may exist. Check the availability calendar above or reach out directly to confirm your date.

What music does DJ AB play?

DJ AB is an open-format DJ fluent in House, Hip-Hop, R&B, Top 40, EDM, Tech House, Afrobeats, Disco, Latin, and more. Sets are fully customized to match the energy and preferences of your event and crowd.

Can I make song requests?

Absolutely. DJ AB welcomes do-not-play lists and special requests in advance. On the night, requests are welcome and worked into the set when they fit the vibe.

How do I get a quote or check availability?
